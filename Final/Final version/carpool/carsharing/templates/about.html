About

Welcome to JHUStudentRide! We are excited to have you in our community. It is well-known that the crime rate in Baltimore is extremely high. It is risky to take random rides from non-authorized drivers. Therefore, we hope to build a platform for mediating drivers and passengers only for JHU students to prevent bad-recorded drivers. The email for registration is limieted to jhu email address to make sure the identity.



How to use ?

(1)  You will first visit our JHUstudentRide homepage. Then, go register for a user account.

(2)  Please fill up the form and be aware of we only accept JHU email. Please do not use other emails. Then, click submit. After successfully registration, you can log into your account from the homepage.

(3)  If you are a driver, click on “I’m a Driver”. If you are a passenger, click on “Pick me up”.

(4)  Drivers can add new trip by filling up the registration for a trip form, including time, departure and destination. Then, drivers will see the registered trip shown in the driver list webpage which can be reached by clicking "Pick me up". If the route you offer is not available anymore, you can click on the cancel bottom to cancel it. The trip will disappear from your driver list webpage. (Note: you cannot cancel the trip if that is not register by you)

(5)  Passengers can see all offered rides in "Pick me up" webpage. Google map search page link is also included, you can click on the address to reach it. Once the time and route that driver provides are match to your plan, you can contact the riders using the jhu email to organize the trip.

(6)  This website is total free. Please enjoy our service!
